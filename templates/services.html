Backer Services
Browse Projects Allows backers to browse and explore live or completed campaigns, view campaign details, read goals, and check creator credibility.
Pledge Projects Enables users to back a project by choosing a reward tier, entering a pledge amount, and completing the payment.
Track Rewards Lets backers track the rewards they’re entitled to, expected shipping dates, delivery status, and any updates or delays from creators.
Bookmark Projects Allows users to bookmark or “heart” projects they’re interested in, follow their progress, and get reminders before campaign deadlines.
Creator Services
Add Project Start your crowdfunding journey by adding a new project. Tell your story and set your funding goals.
Reward Management Lets creators define reward tiers, pricing, estimated delivery dates, limited availability and reward types
Campaign Management Allows creators to monitor campaign progress, edit content, pause campaigns, or extend the duration
Audience Engagement Provides tools for creators to grow and engage their audience.
